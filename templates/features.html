Platform Capabilities

Features Built for Real Care

Behavioral Health Vault provides a simple, auditable workflow for storing behavioral health records — images, documents, and narratives — with Git-backed immutable history.

Patient Records

Upload files and narratives, view your own records, track version history, and ask the AI assistant questions about your recovery journey.

Admin & Clinician View

Review all patient records, moderate content, upload entries on behalf of patients, and access the full audit trail with diff comparisons.

Git-Backed Storage

Every upload is committed to a Git repository, providing cryptographic integrity, full version history, and the ability to compare any two versions side-by-side.

Ask Me AI

A keyword-matching AI assistant that searches across your records — find entries, count uploads, or explore your narrative timeline instantly.

Complete Audit Trail

Every change is timestamped and attributed. Browse commit history, view unified diffs, and download any previous version of any file.

Open Source

Built during Google Summer of Code for the Alaska Division of Behavioral Health. Transparent, community-driven, and free to use.
