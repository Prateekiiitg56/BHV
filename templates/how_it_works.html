How Haven Behavioral Health Vault Works

From sign-up to full record management — secure, versioned, and transparent.

1

Sign Up

Create an account with your email or sign in with Google. Minimal-friction onboarding that keeps your data private.

2

Upload

Share a file — image, document, or text — along with a narrative describing this moment in your recovery journey.

3

Git Commit

Behind the scenes, Haven stores each upload as a Git commit. Every version is immutable, timestamped, and cryptographically verifiable.

4

Review

View your full upload history, compare versions with unified diffs, and download any previous version of any file.

5

Ask Me

Use the AI assistant to search entries by keyword, count records, or get summaries of your entire journey.
