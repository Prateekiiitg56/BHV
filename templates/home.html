Our Mission

Recovery Is a Journey — We Help Document It

In behavioral health, continuity of care depends on reliable records. Haven Health Vault was created during Google Summer of Code for the Alaska Division of Behavioral Health to give patients ownership over their stories while giving clinicians an auditable, tamper-evident record.

Every upload is backed by Git — the same technology that powers global software collaboration — ensuring that no record can be silently altered. Patients see their own growth; providers see a clear, versioned timeline.
